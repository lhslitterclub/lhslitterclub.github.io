What is Litter Club?

Litter club is a club that invites LHS students to go out 1–2 times a month and pick up trash around their community.

  • Low commitment: Come when you can.
  • Visible impact: Cleaner sidewalks, parks, and streets.
  • Exercise: Keep the city, and yourself, healthy.

How do I Participate?

You can participate by either signing up for our email list or by checking the top of this website to see when the next cleanup will be. We send out emails every other week, which include the date, time, and location of the cleanup, as well as a link to the sign-up form. This information is also found at the top of the website. You must fill out the sign-up sheet so that we know how many supplies we will need. Additionally, the sign-up sheet doubles as a permission slip.

  • Step 1: Join the email list (recommended).
  • Step 2: Watch for the cleanup announcement (date/time/location + sign-up).
  • Step 3: Submit the sign-up sheet (also serves as a permission slip).

Can I get volunteer hours for this?

Yes. You can get volunteer hours for this, and if you are in NHS, this counts as your NHS-organized community service. In order to get credit, please sign the volunteer sheet before leaving the cleanup.
